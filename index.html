Hello, and thank you for taking the time to visit my website!  My name is Lydia LeBoeuf, and I am currently a student at Central Michigan University.  I am one of few people that were told by their parents that “maybe you should check out some art classes instead” when I expressed a want to go into dental studies.  My love for the arts has lasted as long as I can remember, and I am thankful for all of the encouragement that I’ve had along the way.  
My love for art has followed me throughout school, and I have regularly experimented with media, style, and technique.  I was also involved with musical and classical theatre for most of my life, along with sports from preschool to my junior year of high school.  I’m a firm believer that the people that I have been surrounded with throughout my life have shaped what I value and my career path.
