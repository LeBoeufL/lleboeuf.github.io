"Bloom Wish"

April 21, 2025
“Bloom Wish’ was a website that I designed in a client group- Clayton Hildebrant, Ava Zaraffano, Emma Corbett, and Sarah Piatek, for ART 335 under David Stairs. The client was a business student, Grace Huynh. Her business was a paper/card company that was centered around using seed paper. They would print wedding advertisements, thank you cards, birthday cards, and even bouquets that could be planted. Their business centered legacy. I was charged with creating the website for the client.
