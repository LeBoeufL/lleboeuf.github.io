"ICU"

April, 2024
This is a silly project, and I loved it. This digital illsutration is inspired by the show 'Bojack Horseman', a netflix original series. The style of the show is simplistic, unnaturalistic, and cartoonish with relatively simple animation and flat colors. I wanted to create a piece to find my style with these characters, depicting them in almost a completely opposite artstyle. This is a show I personallly love very deeply, with an inspiring message that resounded deeply with me. This piece was specifically inspired by the episode 'Free Churro'.
