About Me

Hello! I’m Lydia LeBoeuf, and I am a growing artist studying currently at Central Michigan University, majoring in Graphic Design.

I believe that art is, for some people, a way to express the thoughts and feelings inside your head, whether those be feelings of frustration, inpsiration, happiness, any feeling or image that clings to you until it sinks into the fibers of a canvas.

Background

Awarded my High School Deploma from Menominee High School, MI.

Currently on track to obtain my Bachelor of Fine Arts Degree from Central Michigan University.

Mediums I Work In

  • Graphic/charcoal pencils on paper
  • Oil/Acrylic Paints on canvas
  • Digital Illustration
  • Simple Video Editing
